Autonomous Plant Watering Robot

Developed an autonomous Lego EV3 system meeting the following requirements:

  • Reaches plant pots over 30 cm in height
  • Delivers the correct amount of water to six plants in a row
  • Accurately detects height using an ultrasonic sensor

How: Designed a peristaltic pump with 20% occlusion for steady water flow. Utilized a rack and pinion mechanism to lift and lower the ultrasonic sensor, incorporating a rotating arm for height adjustments. Created 3D-printed components using SolidWorks for precision and tight tolerances. Integrated mechanical systems with C++ scripts for seamless operation.

Why: Selected a peristaltic pump for reliability and ease of prototyping, refining the design through multiple iterations to achieve consistent flow. Used rack and pinion for effective integration with the EV3 environment and to meet height-specific requirements. Leveraged SolidWorks for efficient design iterations.
